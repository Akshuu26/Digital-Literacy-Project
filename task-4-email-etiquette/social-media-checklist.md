 Social Media Checklist: Do’s & Don’ts
 DO’s
1.	Think before you post. Make sure your content is appropriate
2.	Protect your privacy,keep personal information limited
3.	Be respectfull and kind in comments and messages
4.	Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news
5.	Use strong passwords and enable security settings
6.	Balance your time to avoid excessive screen usage
   
 DON’Ts
1.	Don’t share sensitive information
2.	Don’t engage in cyberbullying or negative behavior
3.	Don’t believe everything you see online without checking
4.	Don’t overshare personal life details publicly
5.	Don’t accept friend requests from unknown people blindly
6.	Don’t post when angry or emotional
