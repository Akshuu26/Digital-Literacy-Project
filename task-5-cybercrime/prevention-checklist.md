 Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide

 1. Use strong, unique passwords
Create different passwords for each account and avoid using your name or birthdate.
 2. Enable 2-factor authentication 
Turn on 2FA for email, social media, and payment apps for extra security.
 3. Never share OTPs or passwords
No bank or company will ask for these , keep them private
 4. Protect your UPI PIN 
Never share your UPI PIN or enter it to receive money 
 5. Avoid unknown QR codes 
Do not scan QR codes from strangers , it can lead to money being deducted.
 6. Beware of fake links & phishing
Always check website URLs before logging in or entering details.
 7. Avoid public WiFi for sensitive tasks
Don’t use open networks for banking, shopping   , or logging into important accounts.
 8. Limit what you share online
Avoid posting personal details like phone number, address, or college ID.
 9. Update your apps and phone regularly
Security updates help protect against new cyber threats.
 10.  Log out from shared devices
Always log out when using college computers or cyber cafés.
